Lasagna Recipe

This delicious recipe will blow your socks off!!


Ingredients:

  • Tomato Sauce 200g
  • Pasta sheets x 16
  • Ricotta mixture 400g
  • Mozzerella 220g

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. Pour prepared tomato sauce in the pan
  3. Add cooked noodles
  4. Add a layer of ricotta mixture
  5. Repeat steps 2-4 twice more
  6. Top with mozzerella and BAKE!
