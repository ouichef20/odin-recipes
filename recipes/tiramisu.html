Tiramisu Recipe

Soft and with strong coffee kick this is the perfect end to any meal


Ingredients:

  • cream 500 g
  • mascarpone 250 g
  • sugar 20 g
  • espresso 100 g
  • ladyfingers 30 g
  • cocoa powder 30 g

Directions:

  1. Whip cream and mix together with mascarpone
  2. brush ladyfingers with espresso syrup
  3. line a 9 x 13 dish
  4. add another layer of ladyfingers and espresso syrup
  5. Let cool for 2 hours
  6. Finish with a dusting of cocoa powder Enjoy!
