Cannoli Recipe

Crispy on the outside creamy and dreamy on the insided you wont be able to stop eating this cannoli.


Ingredients:

  • Milk 500 g
  • Egg yolks 20 g
  • Sugar 50 g
  • Cornstarch 20 g
  • Vanilla 2g
  • Cannoli dough x 1 batch

Directions:

  1. Heat milk with Vanilla
  2. mix cornstarch, sugar and egg yolks together
  3. Temper milk into egg mixture
  4. Boil for 2 minutes
  5. Let cool for 2 hours
  6. Fill fried cannoli dough with vanilla pasty cream Enjoy!
